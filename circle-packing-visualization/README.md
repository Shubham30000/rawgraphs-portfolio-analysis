## Project Overview
This Circle Packing visualization represents investment portfolio allocation across different sectors (Technology, Finance, Healthcare, Energy, Consumer, Industrials) and individual assets. The visualization effectively communicates portfolio diversification and relative investment sizes for executive decision-making.

## Visualization Details
- **Chart Type**: Circle Packing
- **Tool Used**: RAWGraphs (https://rawgraphs.io/)
- **Data Structure**: Hierarchical data with sectors and individual assets
- **Total Assets**: 20 investments across 6 sectors

## Key Insights
- Technology sector shows the highest concentration of investments
- Finance sector demonstrates strong diversification across major banks
- Healthcare, Energy, Consumer, and Industrials sectors provide balanced portfolio coverage
- Circle sizes represent relative investment amounts for easy comparison
